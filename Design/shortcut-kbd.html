shortcut keycaps — issue #4

The shortcut hint under Clean Now is currently one line of tiny tertiary monospace text (⌘⇧P toggle · ⌘⇧K clean) — hard to read. Below, each modifier becomes its own keycap (shadcn kbd style), shown inside the real control block so weight and contrast are judged in context. Three named directions — pick one by name.

Paper keycaps with the brand's hard 1px ink border and a hard offset shadow — the same language as the sheet. Highest contrast, most "physical".
