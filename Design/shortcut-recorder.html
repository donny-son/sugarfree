shortcut recorder — make keybindings configurable

The Shortcuts section becomes interactive: each of the three actions (toggle cleanup, clean now, open dashboard) gets a recorder field. Click a field to record a new combo; the field's ✕ clears it; a small reset (↺) restores the default. Each direction below shows the three row states — a bound shortcut, the field while recording, and a cleared (unbound) action — inside the real 320pt sheet. Pick one by name.
