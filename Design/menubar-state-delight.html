menu-bar state + cleanup delight

Two asks: (1) the icon should say at a glance whether Sugarfree is active or paused — today it's only a faint opacity shift. (2) automatic cleanup should feel juicy & delightful, Candy-Crush style. Everything loops; click play to refire.

Brand rule: the menu-bar glyph is normally monochrome — a colored flash during a clean is a deliberate momentary exception (already established by the shipping cue). Smooth motion in the menu bar is best done as a short timer-driven frame sequence; the popover (paper sheet) can animate freely.
