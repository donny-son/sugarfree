notes

The Transforms section sits below “Sugars to strip”, separated by the same hairline rule. It reuses the existing row layout, toggle, and the Cotton accent — no new palette.

Defaults off: converting a table is opinionated and lossy, unlike stripping emphasis.

When enabled, a segmented YAML-style / TOML-style picker appears (mirrors the interval picker style). The mapping is header-keyed list items, lossless for any column count. Output is style (not spec-strict): keys and values are emitted raw, unquoted, for readability.

Toggle the switch or formats above to preview the behavior.
